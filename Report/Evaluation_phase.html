As we can see, in general, when we apply the z-normalization and the z-log normalization, we have better accuracy than without them. Its even more flagrant, when doing the k-fold cross validation.

However, using the neural network is more difficult. Conclusion

In conclusion, applying the z-normalization and the z-score normalization is helpful to improve the accuracy of our classification algorithms. However the gain of applying the normalization is not very much, it is at most 2% more than without the normalization (k-fold cross validation). Even sometimes, applying the z-normalization or the z-log normalization do not always lead to better accuracy (e.g.Decsion tree). The problem of these normalizations is that they are highly dependent on the estimation of the reference intervals. Different estimations, can lead to different results.
